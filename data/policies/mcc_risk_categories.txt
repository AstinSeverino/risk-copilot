MCC RISK CATEGORY CLASSIFICATION

1. STANDARD RISK MCCs
   5411 — Grocery Stores, Supermarkets
       - Expected patterns: high volume, moderate tickets, card-present dominant
       - Typical daily transactions: 200-2000
       - Average ticket: $25-$75
       - Seasonal peaks: holidays, weekends
       - Monitoring focus: ticket size anomalies, refund patterns

   5812 — Eating Places, Restaurants
       - Expected patterns: moderate volume, lower tickets, high card-present
       - Typical daily transactions: 50-300
       - Average ticket: $15-$50
       - Seasonal peaks: weekends, holidays, local events, festivals
       - Monitoring focus: CNP shifts, after-hours transactions

   5541 — Service Stations (Gas)
       - Expected patterns: high volume, bimodal tickets (fuel vs convenience)
       - Typical daily transactions: 100-500
       - Average ticket: $20-$60
       - Seasonal peaks: summer travel, holidays
       - Monitoring focus: pre-authorization amounts, pay-at-pump fraud

2. ELEVATED RISK MCCs
   5999 — Miscellaneous and Specialty Retail
       - Broad category — requires enhanced monitoring
       - Highly variable patterns make anomaly detection harder
       - Monitoring focus: verify actual goods match declared business
       - Requires web presence verification during onboarding

   5944 — Jewelry Stores
       - High-value items susceptible to money laundering
       - Monitoring focus: structuring (just-below-threshold transactions)
       - CTR filing for cash purchases > $10,000

3. HIGH RISK MCCs
   7995 — Betting, Casino Gambling
       - Mandatory Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD)
       - Licensing verification required per jurisdiction
       - Daily transaction monitoring
       - Volume caps for new merchants (90-day probation)
       - High chargeback risk
       - Cross-border gambling restrictions apply
       - Monitoring focus: player funding patterns, velocity, structuring

   5993 — Cigar/Tobacco Stores
       - Age verification requirements
       - Excise tax compliance
       - Cross-border shipping restrictions

   5912 — Drug Stores / Pharmacies
       - DEA licensing verification for controlled substances
       - Prescription fraud monitoring

4. PEER COMPARISON BY MCC
   - Peer groups: all active merchants with same MCC code in same geographic region
   - Metrics compared: daily transaction count, average ticket, total daily volume
   - Z-score calculation: (merchant_value - peer_mean) / peer_std
   - Peer group minimum size: 5 merchants (smaller groups use national benchmarks)

5. CROSS-MCC MONITORING
   - Merchants changing MCC codes: flag for investigation
   - Transaction patterns inconsistent with declared MCC: MCC_MISMATCH reason code
   - Multiple MCCs at same address: enhanced scrutiny for shell companies
   - MCC migration patterns (e.g., 5812→5999): potential business model change or evasion

6. SEASONAL ADJUSTMENT FACTORS
   - Restaurants (5812): +30% during local festivals, +20% during holidays
   - Grocery (5411): +40% during Thanksgiving/Christmas week
   - Gas Stations (5541): +25% during summer travel season
   - Retail (5999): +50% during Black Friday / holiday shopping
   - Gambling (7995): +20% during major sporting events
   - Adjustment applied to baseline before anomaly scoring

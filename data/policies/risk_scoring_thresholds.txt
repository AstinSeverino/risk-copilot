RISK SCORING THRESHOLDS — Internal Policy Document

1. AUTO-APPROVE THRESHOLD
   - Risk probability < 0.5 AND peer volume z-score < 3.0σ
   - Transactions auto-cleared with continued monitoring
   - No LLM investigation triggered
   - Review: quarterly calibration against false negative rate

2. REVIEW THRESHOLD
   - Risk probability 0.5–0.8 without clear contextual explanation
   - Mixed signals: some legitimate factors, some suspicious indicators
   - New merchants (< 180 days) with unusual but not extreme patterns
   - Requires human analyst review within 24 hours
   - SLA: resolution within 72 hours

3. BLOCK THRESHOLD
   - Risk probability > 0.8 AND no legitimate external explanation
   - Evidence of transaction laundering patterns
   - KYB critical flags: sanctions hit, PEP match
   - Business < 30 days old with extreme volume (> 10x peer median)
   - Foreign card percentage > 40% with no business justification
   - Immediate hold on merchant processing
   - Mandatory SAR filing review within 48 hours

4. PEER COMPARISON THRESHOLDS
   - Within 2σ of MCC peer group: normal activity
   - 2–5σ above peers: elevated monitoring
   - 5–10σ above peers: investigation required
   - >10σ above peers: critical alert — immediate review
   - Peer groups defined by MCC code within same geographic region

5. VOLUME GROWTH THRESHOLDS
   - <2x baseline: normal fluctuation
   - 2–5x baseline: flag for review
   - 5–10x baseline: high priority investigation
   - >10x baseline: critical — potential burst-out fraud or laundering

6. TICKET SIZE THRESHOLDS
   - Average ticket within 1.5x MCC median: normal
   - Average ticket 1.5–3x MCC median: monitor
   - Average ticket >3x MCC median: investigate for structuring
   - Maximum single transaction >$10,000: CTR filing required

7. MODEL CONFIDENCE CALIBRATION
   - XGBoost probability is calibrated via Platt scaling
   - IsolationForest anomaly scores normalized to [0, 1]
   - Combined risk score: weighted average (XGBoost 70%, IF 30%)
   - Model retraining: monthly with confirmed fraud labels
   - Shadow deployment: 30-day validation before production cutover
